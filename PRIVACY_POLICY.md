# Privacy Policy for WiFi Invite

**Effective Date:** December 21, 2025

**WiFi Invite** ("the App") is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## 1. Data Collection
We do not collect any personal data, usage statistics, or analytics. The App operates entirely offline on your device.

## 2. WiFi Information
The App accesses your device's WiFi information (SSID) locally to help you generate QR codes. This information is:
*   Processed locally on your device.
*   **Never** transmitted to any external server.
*   **Never** stored by us.

## 3. Network Credentials
When you save network credentials (SSID/Password) within the App:
*   They are stored locally on your device (using `UserDefaults` or `Keychain`).
*   They are used solely for the purpose of generating QR codes.
*   We do not have access to your passwords.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising frameworks.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
